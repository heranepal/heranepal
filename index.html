About Our Charity

Human Education & Relief Association Nepal is a registered, non-profit, non-government, educational and social welfare organization registered under Nepal government and ministry of foreign affairs.

Since its inception, the Organization has been working for relief and rehabilitation of different factions of the society mainly orphans, widows and destitute people.
